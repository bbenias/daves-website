Service Areas in Zimbabwe

Dave's Rock Blasting & Construction provides professional drilling, blasting, concrete breaking, and civil construction services throughout Zimbabwe. While we are based in Bluffhill, we regularly work on projects across the country including:

  • Harare and surrounding areas
  • Bulawayo region
  • Mutare and Eastern Highlands
  • Gweru and Midlands Province
  • Masvingo Province
  • Manicaland Province
  • Mashonaland provinces
  • Matabeleland provinces

If your project is located outside these areas, please contact us to discuss mobilization options. We are equipped to travel to remote locations for projects requiring our specialized drilling and blasting services.

Emergency Services Available

Dave's Rock Blasting & Construction understands that some situations require immediate response. We offer emergency drilling, blasting, and construction services for urgent projects including:

  • Emergency rock stabilization and slope protection
  • Urgent borehole repairs and cleanouts
  • Emergency demolition and concrete breaking
  • Critical infrastructure repairs requiring drilling or blasting
  • Natural disaster response and site remediation

For emergency services, please call our main phone lines and specify that you have an urgent requirement. We will prioritize your request and mobilize our team as quickly as possible.
