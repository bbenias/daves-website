Drilling & Blasting

Dave's Rock Blasting & Construction specializes in professional rock blasting and precision drilling services for construction, mining, and civil engineering projects throughout Zimbabwe. Our experienced blasting engineers and drill operators use modern equipment and proven techniques to ensure safe, efficient, and controlled blasting operations.

Our Drilling & Blasting Services Include:

  • Rock excavation and removal for construction foundations
  • Controlled blasting for site preparation and earthworks
  • Precision drilling for blast holes and anchor installations
  • Quarry development and rock face preparation
  • Underground blasting for tunnels and shafts
  • Vibration monitoring and blast impact assessments

We follow international safety standards and Zimbabwe's regulatory requirements for all blasting operations. Our comprehensive approach includes site surveys, blast design, regulatory compliance, and post-blast inspections to ensure optimal results with minimal environmental impact.

Concrete Breaking

Concrete Breaking Applications:

  • Building demolition and structural removal
  • Foundation breaking and excavation
  • Road and pavement removal
  • Concrete slab breaking for renovations

Our concrete breaking services are ideal for renovation projects, site redevelopment, and infrastructure upgrades where precise, controlled removal is essential. We handle projects of all sizes, from small residential renovations to large-scale commercial demolitions.

Core Drilling

Our precision core drilling services provide clean, accurate holes through concrete, rock, and masonry for a variety of construction and industrial applications. Using diamond-tipped core drill bits and specialized drilling equipment, we deliver precise results with minimal vibration and dust.

Core Drilling Applications:

  • Utility penetrations for plumbing, electrical, and HVAC installations
  • Structural analysis and concrete testing samples
  • Anchor bolt installations and post-tensioning systems
  • Ventilation and ductwork openings
  • Decorative features and architectural details
  • Geological surveys and soil sampling

Core drilling is essential for modern construction projects that require precise penetrations without damaging existing structures. Our experienced operators ensure accurate hole placement and clean edges for all core drilling requirements.
