About Dave's Rock Blasting & Construction

Zimbabwe's trusted partner for professional drilling, blasting, and civil construction services

Who We Are

Dave's Rock Blasting & Construction is Zimbabwe's premier provider of drilling, blasting, concrete breaking, and civil construction services. With years of experience in the construction and mining industries, we have built a reputation for delivering safe, efficient, and reliable solutions to our clients across Zimbabwe.

Our company was founded on the principle that no project is too challenging when you have the right expertise, equipment, and commitment to excellence. From small-scale residential projects to large commercial and industrial developments, we bring the same level of professionalism and attention to detail to every job.

Our Experience & Expertise

With extensive experience across multiple sectors, Dave's Rock Blasting & Construction has successfully completed projects for residential, commercial, industrial, and government clients throughout Zimbabwe. Our team of certified professionals includes experienced blasting engineers, drill operators, and construction specialists who understand the unique challenges of working in Zimbabwe's diverse geological conditions.

Our Core Competencies

  • Rock blasting and controlled demolition for construction projects
  • Precision drilling for foundations, utilities, and infrastructure
  • Concrete breaking and removal for renovations and demolitions
  • Civil construction works including earthworks and site preparation
  • Borehole drilling, maintenance, and rehabilitation
  • Equipment rental with experienced operators

Safety & Compliance

Safety is our top priority at Dave's Rock Blasting & Construction. We strictly adhere to international safety standards and Zimbabwe's regulatory requirements for all drilling, blasting, and construction operations. Our comprehensive safety protocols include:

  • Certified and trained personnel for all specialized operations
  • Regular equipment maintenance and safety inspections
  • Detailed risk assessments for every project
  • Compliance with environmental regulations and best practices
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage for all operations
  • Emergency response procedures and first aid trained staff

We believe that a safe work environment is fundamental to delivering quality results. Our commitment to safety protects our workers, clients, and the communities where we operate.
